Dark Labs

Project information

  • Project date: May 2025
  • Project URL: Github repository
  • Used technologies: Java
  • User guide: User guide

This is a simple game with a text interface. The player finds themselves in a chemical laboratory and their task is to escape. However, before escaping, they must destroy the chemical substance X-87. The game has a total of nine rooms and seven items, three of which are portable.

The player is limited by the need to wear a protective suit in contaminated areas, the need to carry access cards when passing through certain passages, and the capacity of their backpack. The player is therefore forced to frequently put down and pick up items. Locked passages are locked in only one direction, so players may find themselves "locked out" and have to restart the game.
