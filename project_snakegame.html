Snake vs. GhostSnake

Project information

  • Project date: January 2024
  • Project URL: Github repository

A variation of the classic Snake game brings a new dimension and challenges, including the GhostSnake opponent. Controlling the snake is simple using the arrow keys or W, A, S, D, and the goal is to collect apples and avoid GhostSnake. Each apple collected adds 10 points, while GhostSnake, controlled by the computer, can move through your snake and grows five times faster.
